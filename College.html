About College

Previous Kazakh Turkish college Welcome to the modern and intensively developing Zhambyl Innovation Higher College. The college is located in the most historical city of the Republic of Kazakhstan - Taraz. The Higher College was founded in 1993 and is dedicated to improving the performance of highly qualified, competitive specialists in the field of secondary vocational education to solve a complex of economic and social issues, as well as the training of teachers in demand, through the formation of knowledge and world-class skills. The key features of this college are English as the main language of instruction, strict academic requirements in training, and highly qualified teaching staff. To be known in Kazakhstan and recognized worldwide as an educational space for the training of professional and competitive specialists necessary for the high-tech industry and meeting the requirements of society and employers.
